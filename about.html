A trader's toolkit.

So I am new to trading. How do I go from an amateur trader to a professional? Accessorize!

A professional trader assesses his stocks on a number of parameters. If he is looking for a long term investment, a new start-up maybe or a new business unit of some already established company he will go through the balance sheets of the company. The balance sheet sums up the Assets, the Liabilities and the Cashflow of a company thus giving him, the prudent investor an idea of the company's future in the coming months. Such an analysis is called Fundamental Analysis.

Another kind of trader exists in this universe. One who believes that it doesnt matter how well a company is actually doing. The only thing that actually matters is market sentiment. He believes that if everyone thinks the price is going to go up, it will! This kind of traders use various statistical tools to study price movement, volume fluctuation and volatility in the market to assess if the price will rise or fall. This kind of analysis which involves historical prices and their analysis is termed Technical Analysis.
